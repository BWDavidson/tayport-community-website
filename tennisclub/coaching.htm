Tennis Tasters

Tayport Tennis Club has reached an agreement with local Primary Schools to get classes to our club courts for ‘Tennis Taster’ sessions. NO Charge will be made for Tennis Tasters and all tennis equipment will be supplied. Information regarding when these sessions will take place will be distributed via the schools.

Junior Teams

Although Tayport Tennis Club do not presently run junior teams, those wishing to play some team tennis can do so in the local WANT (Wormit & Newport Tennis) teams until teams can be raised from Tayport.

Adult Coaching

Tayport Tennis Club is committed to both growing our membership and providing opportunities for players of all abilities to improve.

We understand that in today’s world, it’s difficult to make regular commitments. With that in mind, we have developed an innovative Adult Coaching Programme which consists of both regular weekday and weekend sessions and our exciting new ‘On Request’ coaching service.

Coaching ‘On Request’ Service

If you are unable to make our regular Wednesday/ Sunday coaching sessions, why not take advantage of our ‘On Request ‘ service.

Simply give 24 hours notice to arrange a coaching session for a group of 4 players, or a one-to-one session, on a day and time that suits you.

  • Group of 4 players @ £5 per person
  • One-to-one coaching @ £25 per hour

That means coaching is available from as little as £5 per hour at a time that suits you!
