How To Find Us

Tayport Tennis Club is located on Queen Street in the town of Tayport, Fife.


View Larger Map

Our tennis club is situated in a quiet area on the outskirts of town. Outside the clubhouse, surrounding the courts, there are a number of benches where you can relax and enjoy the sun while watching a game of tennis.

There is a free car park opposite the courts and the clubhouse provides changing facilities, toilets and a kitchen to cater for those all important after match teas.
