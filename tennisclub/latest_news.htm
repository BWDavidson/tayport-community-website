Junior Coaching - Easter School

Tayport Tennis Club have arranged for an Easter School to run on the week commencing Monday 7th April 2014 - See our Coaching page for more details.

Fundraising - 2014

The tennis season may not have started yet but we have been busy fundraising already. The Annual Quiz was held at Scotscraig Golf Club on Friday 28th February and raised over £300. Many thanks to Scotscraig Golf Club for hosting us and to Derek Ritchie for his excellent work as quiz master.

The Ceilidh was held on Saturday 22nd March and a thoroughly enjoyable evening raised nearly £250.

Our THANKS to all who attended the events and provided raffle prizes. Your support is extremly valuable to us and is much appreciated.
